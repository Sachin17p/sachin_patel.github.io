About Sachin Patel

Sachin is currently working at the Materials Design Lab in the Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering at IIT Madras. DFT-assisted calculations are employed using the VASP code to investigate the impact of vibrational entropy on the thermal stability of various refractory alloys. Our research aims to provide insight into the fundamental properties of materials at the atomic scale, with the ultimate goal of developing advanced materials for various industrial applications.
